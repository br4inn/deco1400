Owner Reviews

The Thrill of the drive by Paul (QLD)

Owning the MR2 Spyder has been an exhilarating experience from day one. As a car enthusiast, I've always appreciated vehicles that offer a pure driving experience, and the MR2 Spyder delivers that in spades. The mid-engine layout provides exceptional balance and handling, making every drive a joy, whether it's a spirited run through twisty mountain roads or a leisurely cruise along the coastline. Inside, the cabin is straightforward and driver-focused. The seating position is low and sporty, with everything you need within easy reach. While the interior materials might not be luxurious, they are functional and durable, reflecting the car's purpose as a driver's machine rather than a grand tourer.

A legacy lives on by Brian (VIC)

The Toyota MR2 Spyder is more than just a car; it’s a legacy that continues to live on, captivating driving enthusiasts like myself with its unique blend of performance, style, and heritage. As an owner from Victoria, Australia, I’ve had the pleasure of experiencing firsthand why this car has such a dedicated following. In an age where many cars are becoming increasingly complex and detached from the driving experience, the MR2 Spyder remains a refreshing reminder of what a sports car should be. It’s a testament to Toyota’s engineering prowess that a car over twenty years old can still deliver such an exhilarating and reliable driving experience. This timeless roadster continues to be a cherished part of my automotive journey, embodying the spirit of driving pleasure in its purest form.
